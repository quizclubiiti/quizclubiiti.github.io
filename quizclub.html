Knowledge, Intelligence, Smarts, Brains or just plain Alertness – call it what you will, you have to admit - it matters. In this spirit, we at the Quiz Club, IIT Indore endeavour to keep the flame of curiosity and the thirst for knowledge alive – one of the noblest pursuits of humankind. The very purpose of our existence! Knowledge is Power! Or to put clichés and lofty thoughts aside...the issue is simple. There’s glory waiting for you but only a question blocks your path...Do you have what it takes? Logical Mastermind? Insatiable Know-it-all? Fancy these titles? Can you beat our Quiz-Masters? Or better still, do you have it in you to be a true Quiz-Master and challenge the rest? "One thing only I know, and that is that I know nothing." – Socrates That was humble coming from Socrates...but can YOU do better?
